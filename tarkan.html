Tarkan
Singer, song-writer, music producer.

Tarkan is undisputedly one of the Turkey’ s greatest representatives, with far reaching success throughout Europe, Russia, Turkic Republics, Middle East, South America and Australia.

He is a World Music Award winning artist who has sold over 15 million records and charted top 40 singles for every hit released from 1992 to the present day.

Tarkan’s lasting impression is undeniable – as both he and his music have been woven into the rich and intricate fabric of Turkish culture. His music has always flowed to the beat of pop culture with his heart drumming to its ever-changing pulse.

•Tarkan’s debut album Yine Sensiz was released by Istanbul Plak Ltd. (the record company that released the first four of his albums) in 1992. The album sold 800,000 units.
•His second album Aacayipsin was released in 1995. 2,300,000 units sold resulted in his first #1 single, the title track of the album Şıkıdım internationally known as Shikidim and established Tarkan as a force to be noticed in the world of Turkish music.
•The Aacayipsin tour consisted of 85 live concerts throughout Europe & Turkey where 26 of them were stadium performances.
•The third album Ölürüm Sana was released in 1997 and reached 2,400,000 units sold nationwide.
•The hit, Şımarık of Ölürüm Sana, and popularly known as Kiss Kiss exploded onto the world market and topped the Billboard Charts Hot 100 List selling alone 1,000,000 units.
•A sold out European tour followed with visits in 17 cities in 8 countries.
•Hits from the third album got heavy airplay on international music channels including MCM & MTV Europe.
•In 1998, PolyGram France licensed Ölürüm Sana, re-releasing it internationally as a compilation album entitled Tarkan in over 50 countries.
•The album sold over 3,500,000 units worldwide – certified platinum in Mexico and gold in France, Holland, Germany, Belgium, Luxembourg, Sweden, and Columbia.
•Besides the very famous English versions of Şımarık by Stella Soleil and cover of the same by Holly Valance both under the title Kiss Kiss, there were many other covers of the song in English and various languages -Russian, Ukranian, Spanish, Korean, Hungarian, German, Serbian, Persian, Greek, Hebrew, Japanese, Dari, Indonesian, Telugu (Indian), Portuguese, Bulgarian, Romanian and Slovenian. Şıkıdım was released as the second single after the success of Şımarık which has also been covered in many languages.
•To honor his successes, Tarkan was awarded a World Music Award, at Monaco’s 1999 WMA.
•Following his appearances in Turkey, Tarkan toured in over 17 cities in Europe, with additional stops in South America.
Karma, the fourth album was released in August 2001.
•Kuzu Kuzu, the first single off the album, released in May 2001, held the #1 position for three months and sold alone 600,000 units.
•Karma’ s success, with over 2,000,000 units sold, extended to Russia where it influenced a surprising change in the landscape of the Russian music market.
•Following this triumph, being requested to record the official song of the Turkish National Soccer Team, Tarkan released Bir Oluruz Yolunda re-writing Taş from Karma.
•Furthermore, Hüp from Karma was used in the documentary film produced by the Turkish Ministry of Tourism, which also rewarded him for his effective contribution to represent Turkey.
•Dudu, his 5th album, released in Summer 2003 by HITT Music, record company owned by Tarkan, garnered unprecedented acclaim for Tarkan and sold over 1,500,000 units in Turkey. Another record-breaking sale number has been reached in Russia resulting in an award for the album’s title track Dudu.
•The release of his first English album Come Closer in 2006 under Universal Music Germany marked a new era for Tarkan. Singing in English exposed Tarkan’s music to a new world of listeners with sold out concert tours round the globe.
•Come Closer is a globally recorded album, produced in New York, Los Angeles, London and Istanbul and is a work of Tarkan’s collaboration with world class composers & musicians, as well as guest appearances by artists and producers such as Wyclef Jean/Why Don’t We (Aman Aman), Brian Kierulf and Josh Schwartz aka KNS, David Werner, Devrim Karaoglu, the Jettsonz, Lester Mendez, Ozan Çolakoğlu , Pete Martin and Supaflyas.
•In 2007, Tarkan unveiled his new album Metamorfoz (Metamorphosis) and despite the music industry’s overall decline in physical sales, close to half a million units were sold upon release.
•Always at the forefront, Tarkan became the most downloaded artist on TTNetMüzik -Turkey’s digital music market leader back then- where hits from Metamorfoz reached 1,500,000 initial and 10,000,000 of total downloads.
•Metamorfoz was followed by the release of the Metamorfoz Remixes album in 2008, which had Tarkan collaborating with world renowned DJ’s, such as Tїesto.
•The release of Metamorfoz in Russia in 2008 was followed by Tarkan’s initial license deal for the Middle East market.

Besides the successes in his music career, being an environmental activist, Tarkan continues to devote his time to causes that matter to him the most, including his involvement in the Doğa Derneği (Nature Association), as a spokesperson raising awareness for environmental issues. His 2008 single Uyan, motivated fellow artists in Turkey to join the cause. Tarkan has continued in his philanthropic activism through his partnership with PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals), as the face of their campaigns, inspiring not only his musical peers but also his devoted fans, to stand up for change. The 8th studio album Adımı Kalbine Yaz, released in Summer 2010 by DMC, has been a landmark manifestation in Tarkan’ s career resulting in award winning number of physical sales of close to half a million units in national market accompanied by a record breaking level of millions of digital downloads. Known for his deep love and devotion to Turkish Classical Music, Tarkan released Ahde Vefa in March 2016 from his label, HITT Music. Consisting of 13 memorable songs of Turkish Classical Music, this highly anticipated album has won two well-deserved awards.
